Introduction

 Founded in 2004 to fulfill the Lord Jesus Christ's Great Commission of going into all the world and preaching the Gospel to every creature and teaching Christians to witness for Christ (Mark 16:15-20, Matthew 28:18-20), Soulwinners Ministries International both travels internationally, preaching and teaching the Gospel of Jesus Christ, in Gospel crusades, revival meetings, evangelism training seminars, Bible colleges, ministry training conferences, pastors' evangelism training conferences, church services, on college campuses, at public events, and wherever people are present, and, nationally, trains Soulwinners Fellowship, a small Book-of-Acts evangelism training center house-church of believers founded by the ministry in East Lansing, Michigan, in Biblical Holiness and Holy Ghost Preaching. Internationally, the ministry has preached and taught the Gospel in 16 nations, and on 4 continents of the earth (the United States of America; Canada; the Bahamas; Ghana, West Africa; Nigeria, West Africa; Germany; Poland; Czech Republic; Slovakia; Austria; India; Tanzania, East Africa; Kenya, East Africa; Zambia, Southern Africa; Ethiopia, East Africa; Malawi, Central Africa). Nationally, the ministry has preached and taught the Gospel in the open air on 100+ college campuses, in 30 states. We invite you to view the rest of this website, and join us at Soulwinners Ministries International as we TAKE THIS WORLD FOR JESUS!!!
